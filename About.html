About Us

The Equilibrium is a student-led platform dedicated to making economics more accessible, engaging, and relevant for other students. We provide a space where students can publish blogs, reviews, and research, helping them grow as thinkers and writers. By giving students a voice, we aim to break down stereotypes about who "belongs" in economics and highlight work from underrepresented groups. We are committed to build an inclusive community that reflects the diversity of the world around us.
